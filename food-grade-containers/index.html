FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 Compliance

Polypropylene under FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 is approved for direct food contact applications including:

  • Produce bins and crates — direct berry, citrus, leafy green contact
  • Meat containers — poultry, pork, beef processing
  • Dairy — cheese aging containers, milk transport
  • Dry goods — grain, flour, sugar, spice packaging
  • Frozen food — rated to -10°F without embrittlement

Why PP Over Other Plastics for Food

PP is the preferred food-contact plastic for a reason:

  • Higher heat resistance than LDPE or HDPE — autoclave-rated grades available
  • Non-porous surface — no bacterial biofilm formation
  • FDA and USDA accepted — no special certification process required
  • BPA-free by nature — PP contains no bisphenol compounds
